Chronic Health

Flexible Daily Health Tracker

Chronic Health is the easiest way to create a holistic health journal that is completely customized to your specific needs.

Chronic health is inspired by the evergreen tree and the goal of being healthy year-round.

Features

  • Daily survey with smart carry-forward logic
  • Track symptoms, treatments, side effects, and illnesses
  • Customize how each item is tracked (frequency, severity, etc.)
  • Calendar view of your health history
  • Reports and charts for spotting patterns
  • Export as a PDF to share with healthcare providers
  • Export as CSV for data analysis
  • Fully offline — all data stays on your device
  • No account required

AI

If you would like to chat with an AI about the health data you've collected, you can export the data you want to give to the AI. This skill file gives the AI everything they need to understand what the Chronic Health data is and how to interpret it. This can be useful for combining Chronic Health data with other data sources like fitness trackers, making visualizations, or doing research on potential symptom causes.

Once you give your data to an AI system, it cannot be undone. Make sure you trust the AI with your data.
