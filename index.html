Chronic Health

Flexible Daily Health Tracker

Chronic Health is the easiest way to create a holistic health journal that is completely customized to your specific needs.

Features

  • Daily survey with smart carry-forward logic
  • Track symptoms, treatments, side effects, and illnesses
  • Customize how each item is tracked (frequency, severity, etc.)
  • Calendar view of your health history
  • Reports and charts for spotting patterns
  • Export as a PDF to share with healthcare providers
  • Export as CSV for data analysis
  • Fully offline — all data stays on your device
  • No account required
